With more than six decades supporting government clients and programs, Jacobs' experience includes the design, construction, management and operations of virtually all types of facilities and infrastructure. As a strategic partner to clients, we keep abreast of evolving government criteria, stay invested in technological advances, and provide cost-effective sustainable solutions for old and new facilities worldwide. Our portfolio includes repeat work from all branches of the military, the non-military government agencies, and the intelligence community.

Defense

Jacobs provides our depth of services to defense clients through multimillion-dollar framework contracts for real estate consulting; advance planning; asset management; architecture; interior design; workplace solutions; all disciplines of engineering; program & construction management;commissioning services; and construction. Non-Defense
